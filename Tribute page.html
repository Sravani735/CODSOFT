A Glimpse into His Life...

Dr. Kalam's life was a testament to his passion for learning and his dedication to the nation. He remained humble and kind, inspiring generations to come.

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu.
  • 1954 - Graduated in Physics from the University of Madras.
  • 1960 - Graduated in Aerospace Engineering from the Madras Institute of Technology.
  • 1961 - Joined DRDO as a scientist.
  • 1969 - Joined ISRO for Space Research.
  • 1980 - Became the project director for India's first indigenous satellite program.
  • 1980 - Contributed to the development of several indigenous missiles for India like Agni and Prithvi.
  • 1981 - Awarded Padma Bhushan.
  • 1990 - Awarded Padma Vibhushan.
  • 1992 - Chief Advisor for India's nuclear program.
  • 1997 - Awarded Bharat Ratna.
  • 2002 - Became the 11th President of India.
  • 2015 - Passed away while doing what he loved the most, addressing students.

"Dream, Dream, Dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
